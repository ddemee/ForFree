Kindness is contagious!

Your help may mean more to someone, than you think!

FOR FREE is a free application for free items to help people.



Users can use free calls and free messages to contact and get help from each other.​ The purpose of the application is to help each other and do good, not only when we are asked or when we see that our help is needed for someone, but always, everywhere and, most importantly, with what we can. Your help may mean more to someone than you think! With kindness we will make the world a better place.
